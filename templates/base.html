Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025  |  GrabBite Pvt. Ltd.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in and understand how you interact with the site.

Types of Cookies We Use
  • Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function. These include session cookies that keep you logged in and security tokens that protect your account. These cannot be disabled.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings such as delivery address, language preference and notification options.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users navigate our platform, which pages are most visited and where users encounter issues. We use anonymised data from these cookies to improve our service.
  • Marketing Cookies: Allow us to show you relevant promotions and offers based on your browsing behaviour. These are only set with your consent.
Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services including Google Analytics (analytics), Razorpay (payments) and Google Maps (delivery tracking) that may set their own cookies. These parties have their own privacy policies governing cookie use.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block, delete or receive alerts when cookies are set. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent GrabBite from functioning correctly — you will not be able to log in or place orders.

To opt out of analytics cookies, visit Google Analytics Opt-out.

Cookie Retention

Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain for up to 12 months or until you delete them. You can clear all cookies at any time through your browser settings.

Contact

Questions about our use of cookies? Email privacy@grabbite.com.

Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2025  |  GrabBite Pvt. Ltd.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • Your order was not delivered within 90 minutes of placement
  • Items were missing from your order
  • Food received was significantly different from what was described
  • Food arrived in an inedible or spoiled condition
  • You were charged incorrectly (duplicate charges, wrong amount)
  • The restaurant cancelled your order after payment was collected
2. Non-Refundable Situations
  • Change of mind after the order is placed and accepted
  • Incorrect delivery address provided by the customer
  • Customer unavailable at delivery address after 2 delivery attempts
  • Dissatisfaction with taste (subjective preference)
  • Delays caused by extreme weather or circumstances beyond our control
3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, raise a complaint within 24 hours of order delivery or expected delivery time through:

  • The "My Orders" section — select the order and tap "Report Issue"
  • Email: support@grabbite.com with your order ID and photos
  • Phone: +91 80001 23456 (available 8 AM – 10 PM)
4. Refund Processing Time
  • GrabBite Wallet: Instant (within 30 minutes)
  • UPI / Net Banking: 1–3 business days
  • Credit / Debit Card: 5–7 business days
  • EMI transactions: 7–10 business days
5. Partial Refunds

In cases where only some items are affected (e.g. one item missing from a multi-item order), GrabBite will process a partial refund equivalent to the value of the missing or incorrect item, including any applicable taxes on that item.

6. Promotions & Coupons

If a refund is processed for an order where a coupon was used, the refunded amount reflects the discounted price paid, not the original price. Coupons are not reinstated after a refund.

7. Escalation

If your refund request is denied and you believe this is incorrect, you may escalate to our Grievance Officer at grievance@grabbite.com. We commit to responding within 48 hours.
