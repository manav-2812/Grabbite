Our Story

GrabBite was founded in Bengaluru in 2021 with one simple mission: make great food accessible to everyone, fast. What started as a small team of 5 passionate foodies has grown into India's most loved food delivery app.

We believe food is more than sustenance — it's culture, comfort, and community. Every day, we connect millions of people with the meals they crave, while helping thousands of restaurant partners grow their business.

Our Values

Speed

We obsess over delivery times. Our real-time tracking ensures your food arrives hot and on time, every time.

Safety

Every restaurant partner is verified. Food quality and hygiene standards are non-negotiable.

Community

We support local restaurants, create delivery jobs, and build stronger food communities across India.

Sustainability

Eco-friendly packaging, electric delivery fleet, and zero food waste initiatives are at our core.

Customer First

Every decision we make starts with a simple question: does this make the customer experience better?

Innovation
